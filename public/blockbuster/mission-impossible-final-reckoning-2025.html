The Last Hunt

Eight films deep, the Mission: Impossible franchise reaches its intended finale. The practical stunt work, shot across 13 countries, is genuinely jaw-dropping: a submarine sequence, an aerial dogfight, and a finale aboard a biplane that makes the train sequence from Dead Reckoning look modest. Tom Cruise, now in his 60s, remains cinema's greatest living action star.

"Nobody does it like Tom Cruise, and the franchise has the grace to end at its peak — pure, exhilarating cinema."

— Total Film

The Price of Ambition

The film's narrative stumbles under its own mythology — the Entity AI plotline finds resolution, but the sheer number of characters returning creates pacing issues. A $400M budget, a $523M gross, and a box office loss for Paramount — but for audiences, a triumphant finale to one of cinema's great action franchises.

The Verdict

Flawed, overlong, impossibly expensive, and yet undeniably thrilling. Cruise goes out a legend. The mission, however improbable, is accomplished.
