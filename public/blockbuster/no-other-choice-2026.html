Park Chan-wook Unleashed

At 97% on RT, No Other Choice has landed as one of the most acclaimed films of the 2025–2026 award season. Park's first film since Decision to Leave is a tonal high-wire act — a revenge thriller that keeps finding genuine comedy in its escalating violence, like watching a Rube Goldberg machine built entirely from human suffering and perfect timing. Lee Byung-hun plays Ko Guk, a mild-mannered accounting executive who discovers his entire career was built on embezzlement by his superiors — and decides, with meticulous precision, to dismantle their lives one carefully engineered disaster at a time.
